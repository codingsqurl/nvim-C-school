## How to Use This File

1. **Pick your school** based on tone and focus
2. **Start at Beginner** — complete all 8 tasks in that milestone
3. **Advance linearly** — Beginner → Intermediate → Advanced → Expert
4. **Cross-train** — tasks from other schools count as bonus credit
5. **Track progress** — mark completed tasks with `[x]`
